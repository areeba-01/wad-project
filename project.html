Our Story

Baked with intention

The Pastry Palette began in a small Islamabad kitchen in 2021, when founder Areeba Amir started baking croissants for her neighbours on Sunday mornings. Word spread as butter and flour tend to do.

Today we're a beloved institution, but we've never lost sight of what matters: slow processes, honest ingredients and pastry that makes you close your eyes for a moment.
